Bitcoin Consultancy

Bitcoin refers to both a digital currency and the open source software allowing transactions to be made within the bitcoin economy. There is already a 40 million dollar economy surrounding bitcoin and at the time of writing bitcoin has appeared in Time magazine's online article section and a fully distributed Forbes magazine article within the last month. Half of the eight exchanges surrounding bitcoin have opened in the past 3 months. And the value of bitcoin has increased dramatically as awareness of its uses and future potential permeates society.

While bitcoin is rapidly expanding economy with merchant sites of every kind. Our interest is core Bitcoin development.
